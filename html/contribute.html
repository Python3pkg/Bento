How to contributeÂ¶

Although bento is still in early stages, there are several ways to contribute to the project effectively if you are interested:

  • Try the convert command on as many packages as possible, and report failures
  • Report bento bugs

You may also look at the TODO list, and take a shot at the missing features. As bento design is still in flux, please discuss any non-trivial feature on the Mailing List.
